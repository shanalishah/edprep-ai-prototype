## üìù **Sample Test Prompts**

### **Task 2 Prompts (Opinion Essays)**
1. "Some people think that technology has made our lives more complicated. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
2. "Environmental problems are too big for individual countries to deal with. We have reached the stage where the only way to protect the environment is for ordinary people to take action at a global level. To what extent do you agree or disagree?"
3. "Some people believe that children should be allowed to stay at home and play until they are six or seven years old. Others believe that it is important for young children to go to school as soon as possible. Discuss both views and give your own opinion."

### **Task 1 Prompts (Data Description)**
1. "The chart below shows the percentage of male and female students studying six different subjects at a UK university in 2008. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant."
2. "The graph below shows the number of university graduates in Canada from 1992 to 2007. Summarise the information by selecting and reporting the main features, and make comparisons where relevant."
